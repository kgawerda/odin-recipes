Mojito

Description

Mojito is a traditional Cuban highball. It's also one of my favorite drinks so make it. Now.

Ingredients

  • Mint leaves
  • Half of a medium lime
  • 2 tablespoons demerara sugar
  • 50 ml white rum
  • Sparkling water to top
  • Ice, ice, more ice. I see that I'm icy!

Steps

  1. Place mint leaves and 1 lime wedge into a sturdy glass. Use a muddler and crush to release mint oils and lime juice.
  2. Add remaining lime wedges and 2 tablespoons sugar, and muddle again to release the lime juice. Do not strain the mixture.
  3. Fill the glass almost to the top with ice. Pour in rum and fill the glass with sparkling water.
  4. Stir, taste, and add more sugar if desired.
  5. Drink and repeat until drunk.
